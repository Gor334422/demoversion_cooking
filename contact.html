Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How to register?

Go to the registration page, fill out the form and confirm your email. You're ready to use all site features.

❓ How to enroll in a course?

Select the course you're interested in, click "Enroll" and follow the payment instructions. Access is provided immediately after payment.

❓ How to place orders in the shop?

Add items to cart, fill in delivery address and choose payment method. Track delivery by order number.

❓ What is the delivery cost?

Delivery in Moscow: free for orders over 1000₽. Regional: 299₽. Delivery is completed within 3-5 business days.

❓ Are there discounts?

Yes! Subscribe to our newsletter and get 15% off your first order. Regular customers have special offers.

❓ How to cancel subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time in "My Account" > "Subscriptions". Refunds are not provided for the spent period.
