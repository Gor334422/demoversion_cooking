## 🚀 How to Use

### For End Users (Visitors)

#### 1. **Viewing the Site**
- Visit `index.html` - entire site is now in English
- All navigation menus are in English
- All recipes, courses, and content are in English

#### 2. **Creating a Profile**
- Click the avatar icon in the top-right navigation bar
- You'll be taken to your account page (`account.html`)
- Fill in your profile information
- Click **"Change Photo"** to upload a profile picture

#### 3. **Profile Data Persistence**
When you create/update your profile:
- Your avatar photo is saved
- Your name is saved
- Your email is saved
- Your phone number is saved
- Your address is saved

**All data is automatically saved** - no manual action needed!

#### 4. **Avatar Synchronization**
Your avatar automatically appears:
- ✅ On your account profile page
- ✅ In the navigation bar on every page
- ✅ On index.html and account.html
- ✅ Across all browser tabs you have open
- ✅ Even after you close and reopen the browser

## 📞 Support Information

**Issue: Avatar not showing?**
- Clear browser cache
- Try uploading image again
- Check browser console for errors

**Issue: Data not saving?**
- Enable localStorage in browser settings
- Try disabling browser extensions
- Use a different browser

**Issue: Not syncing across tabs?**
- Verify both tabs are on same domain
- Check browser console
- Refresh the other tab
