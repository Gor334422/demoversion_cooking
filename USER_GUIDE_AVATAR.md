# Quick User Guide - Avatar & Profile Management

## 🎯 How to Use the New Avatar System

### Step 1: Access Your Account
- Click the avatar icon in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
- You'll be taken to your account page

### Step 2: Change Your Profile Photo
1. Click the **"Change Photo"** button on your profile page
2. Select an image from your computer
3. The photo will be uploaded and saved automatically

### Step 3: Your Photo Will Appear On:
- ✅ The avatar icon in the navigation bar (index.html)
- ✅ Your account profile page (account.html)
- ✅ All other pages you visit with the navigation bar
- ✅ Persists even after you close the browser!

---

## 📝 Updating Your Profile

1. Scroll to **"Edit Profile"** section
2. Update your information:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Phone Number
   - Address
3. Click **"Save Changes"**
4. Your information is saved and will appear when you return

---

## 💾 What Gets Saved?

When you use your account:
- ✅ Profile photo (as image file)
- ✅ Full name
- ✅ Email address
- ✅ Phone number
- ✅ Address

**All data is saved in your browser** and will be available the next time you visit, even if you:
- Close your browser
- Clear recent history
- Come back days later

---

## 🔄 Cross-Device Synchronization

**Note**: Currently, your avatar and profile data are stored locally on your computer. If you:
- Use a different browser
- Use a different computer
- Use your phone

...you'll see a new default avatar. To access your profile across devices, server-side account syncing would need to be added in the future.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Where is my photo stored?
**A**: Your photo is stored in your browser's local storage. It's never sent to a server unless you set up an account backend.

### Q: Can I have multiple photos?
**A**: Currently, you can only have one profile photo. You can change it anytime by uploading a new one.

### Q: What image formats are supported?
**A**: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and most common image formats are supported.

### Q: What's the maximum file size?
**A**: Technically unlimited, but images over 5MB may cause performance issues. Try to keep images under 2MB.

### Q: Will my data be deleted?
**A**: Your data persists until you:
- Clear browser cookies/cache specifically for this site
- Use "Clear Browsing Data" and select this domain
- Manually delete your profile

### Q: Can I sign out?
**A**: Yes! Click **"Sign Out"** button on your profile page. This will remove your login data but keep your avatar saved.

## 🚀 Best Practices

1. **Use a clear, recognizable profile photo** - Choose a photo that represents you well
2. **Keep your profile info updated** - Ensure your contact information is current
3. **Don't share sensitive data** - Address should be just city/country if preferred
4. **Use a common image format** - JPG or PNG works best
5. **Clear photo of your face** - Helps other users recognize you

If you encounter any issues:

3. Clear browser cache and reload
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if problems persist
